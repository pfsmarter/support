Quick Help

For quick assistance with common issues in PFSmarter applications, check out these tips:

General Issues

  • **App crashes?** Try reinstalling the app and make sure your iOS is up to date.
  • **Incorrect results?** Check the in-app settings for any rounding or precision options.
  • **Still an issue?** Email us with screenshots or a detailed description of the problem.

Status & Response

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 3–5 business days. Please note that service availability may vary on weekends and holidays.
